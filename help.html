Angel One Full Registration

📌 Note: Candidate ke paas Aadhaar linked mobile number aur PAN card hona chahiye.
1
Basic Registration Link open karke Name, Mobile Number aur City enter karein. OTP verify karke 'Start' par click karein.
2
Email Verification Apni Email ID dalein ya Google account se login karein. Email par aaya OTP verify karein.
3
PAN & DOB Details PAN card number aur Date of Birth (as per PAN) sahi-sahi fill karein.
4
Digital Locker (KYC) DigiLocker page khulega. Aadhaar number dalkar 'Next' karein, OTP verify karein aur documents share karne ke liye 'Allow' par click karein.
5
Bank Account Details Apna Bank Account number aur IFSC code enter karein. Isse aapka account verify hoga.
6
E-Sign Process NSDL ka page khulega. Aadhaar number dalkar check-box tick karein aur Mobile OTP se final E-Sign karein.

Upstox Full Registration

📌 Note: Upstox mein mobile number aur email verification pehle hota hai.
1
Sign Up & OTP Mobile number enter karein, OTP verify karein. Uske baad "Continue with Google" par click karke email link karein.
2
KYC Personal Details PAN Card details aur DOB dalein. Iske baad Gender, Marital Status aur Annual Income select karein.
3
Digital Signature & Selfie Screen par apna signature ungli se karein. Uske baad ek saaf Selfie capture karein jisme face clear dikhe.
4
Bank Linking Apna Bank Account Number aur IFSC dalein. Upstox aapke bank mein ₹1 bhej kar verify karega.
5
Final E-Sign 'E-sign with Aadhaar OTP' par click karein. NSDL portal par Aadhaar dalkar final verification poora karein.
